Quesadilla

Description

Join us in creating a cheesy treat that all will enjoy. This recipe is not only quick and easy to make but it is a delicious meal!

Ingredients

  • Tortilla
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat pan
  2. Lay out tortilla and cover half of it with cheese(shredded preferred
  3. Fold the tortilla, covering it and waiting for cheese to melt
  4. Once cheese has began to melt, flip the quesadilla
  5. Toast to your preference and Enjoy!
