Strawberry Spinach Salad

Description

We will be making a declicious and healthy meal. With a few Ingredients we are able to create a flavorful straberry spinach salad!

Ingredients

  • 2 bunches spinach, rinsed and torn into bite-size pieces
  • 4 cups sliced strawberries
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • 2 tablespoons sesame seeds

Steps

  1. Toss spinach and strawberries together in a large bowl.
  2. Whisk oil, sugar, vinegar, sesame seeds, poppy seeds, and paprika together in a medium bowl.
  3. Pour over the spinach and strawberries, and toss to coat.
