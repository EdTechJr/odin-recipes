Sandwich

Description

This recipe is for one of the greatest sandwiches ever created, not only is it a declicious meal but it is also very simple to make

Follow along as we make a BLT sandwich in a few easy steps!

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Tomato
  • Bacon
  • Lettuce
  • Mayonnaise

Steps

  1. Toast two slices of bread
  2. Add mayonnaise to one or both slices of bread
  3. Add lettuce to the first slice of bread
  4. Add one of two slices of tomato
  5. Add cooked bacon
  6. Add the final slice of bread on-top
  7. Enjoy!
